All About

Sea Turtles

Fact File

  • Scientific Name: Testudines
  • Speed: ~35 kph
  • Life Span: 40-60 years

    • FUN FACT!

    Fully grown sea turtles are herbivores and like to hang around
    coral reefs to scrape off seagrass and algae. Hatchlings ,however,
    are omnivorous.

    Turtles are fantastic and unique creatures who live in almost every continent exept antarctica.Turtle shells are made mostly of bone;
    the upper part is the domed carapace, while the underside is the flatter plastron or belly-plate.Its outer surface is covered in scales
    made of keratin.Sadly turtles are an endangered spieces with only one-million, one-hundred and thirty-three thousand sea turtles left.
